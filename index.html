About

How It All Started

I’ve always been fascinated by how things work from the gears inside machines to the logic behind the code that powers our digital world. My journey started in Mechanical Engineering, where I learned to think like a builder to see problems not as obstacles, but as puzzles waiting to be solved. Engineering taught me precision, patience, and the beauty of structure. That curiosity eventually led me into the world of Computer Essentials and Engineering, where I began to see technology not just as tools, but as a bridge connecting creativity and function, people and purpose. It was there that I discovered something that completely changed my path: Frontend Development. Building interfaces felt like a natural evolution of my engineering mindset still solving problems, still crafting systems, but now creating experiences that people could actually feel and interact with. Today, I channel that same curiosity and craftsmanship into designing and developing clean, responsive, and meaningful digital experiences that make users feel at home the moment they land on a page. Every project I take on is more than just code to me, it’s a reflection of my growth, my standards, and my commitment to delivering top-notch, thoughtful, and user-focused work. I believe great design doesn’t just look good, it feels right.

And Now

I blend beautiful interfaces, performance engineering and product thinking to deliver websites that convert. Whether it's redesigning a landing funnel, building a progressive web app, or building SEO-friendly markup. I focus on outcomes: more leads, more sales, less friction.
